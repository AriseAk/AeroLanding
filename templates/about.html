About Us
Welcome to AeroBMSCE Aeromodelling Club—a community for aviation, drones, and RC plane enthusiasts. We inspire and empower members to explore aerodynamics and aerial robotics through hands-on learning. Our mission is to help you design, build, and fly innovative aero-vehicles with support from experienced mentors. We organize workshops, training sessions, and events for all skill levels, fostering teamwork and creativity. Members gain access to advanced equipment, simulators, and building materials to turn ideas into reality. We host competitions and showcases, offering opportunities to display your creations and compete at various levels. Our inclusive club welcomes everyone, encouraging knowledge-sharing and lifelong friendships. Join us to develop practical skills, connect with like-minded individuals, and reach new heights in aeromodelling. Be part of a supportive, innovative, and fun community—where your passion for flight truly takes off.
